This area currently is not only unpleasant to walk through at the moment but also very dangerous. There is very little pedestrian infrustructure. In fact, one of the two connections between Corona and Flushing force pedestrians to cross the highway TWICE!

This area is a major throughfare to connect Eastern Queens to Western Queens. There is no cycling or microbility infrustructure. There's even no CitiBike at CitiField.
